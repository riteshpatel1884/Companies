Discount Calculator

Problem Statement: You are given a purchase amount. Based on the amount, a discount is applied as follows:


Discount Rules
If amount < 1000 → 5% discount
If amount ≥ 1000 and < 5000 → 10% discount
If amount ≥ 5000 → 15% discount


Task: Calculate the final payable amount after applying the discount.


Input Format: A single integer: amount (total purchase value)


Output Format: Print the final amount after discount,
Rounded to 2 decimal places
