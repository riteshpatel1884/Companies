Eligible Laptops for Meeting

Problem Statement: A company has laptops with different battery charging capacities. For an urgent meeting, only laptops with charging capacity greater than or equal to a required minimum value K can be used.


Task: Find how many laptops are eligible for the meeting.


Input (implied from problem)
An integer n → number of laptops
An array of n integers → charging capacities
An integer K → minimum required capacity


Output: Print the count of laptops

Ex: 
5
3 6 8 2 10
5

Count = 3 (6,8,10)
