You are given two integers n1 and n2.

Task:
- Find the n1-th prime number
- Find the n2-th prime number
- Compute their product
- Subtract 1 from the result

Output: Return the final value after subtraction.

Input:
n1 = 4
n2 = 5

Prime sequence:
2, 3, 5, 7, 11, ...

4th prime = 7
5th prime = 11

Calculation:
7 × 11 = 77
77 - 1 = 76

Output: 76
